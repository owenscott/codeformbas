Ten Oldest Colleges in Oxford

Below is a list of the ten oldest colleges in Oxford, from oldest to youngest.

  • St Edmund Hall (1226)
  • University College (1249)
  • Balliol College (1263)
  • Merton College (1264)
  • Hertford College (1282)
  • Exeter College (1314)
  • Oriel College (1326)
  • The Queen's College (1341)
  • New College (1379)
  • Lincoln College (1427)
